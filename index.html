Why Qcash?

We empower companies to provide credit facilities and salary advances to employees

Quick business loans can also be offered to members of a closed group such as credit unions and cooperative savings

Our systems are fully automated and work 24hrs a day, allowing emergency loan requests at any time of the day or night.

Here is how it works
